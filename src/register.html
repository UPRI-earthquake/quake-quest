Online Registration

Click the link to redirect you to the Registration Form.

Who May Join?

Currently enrolled Senior High School students (Grades 11 & 12). Each school may send one representative team composed of 3 SHS students and 1 Faculty member.

General Guidelines

  1. Answer the registration form at https://bit.ly/2024QQ-RegForm. Kindly designate one representative from your team (coach or student) to complete and submit the form.
  2. Ready the following requirements. You may submit the supplementary documents through the registration form or email them to quakequest.upri@up.edu.ph. Please indicate [Registration - School name - Supplementary Documents Submission] in the subject line of your email.
    1. Proof of enrollment (e.g., School ID or Certificate of Registration) showing that the students are currently enrolled in Grade 11 or 12.
    2. Proof/certificate of employment of coach/adviser signed by the highest authority of the school stating that the faculty member is a full-time/permanent faculty member teaching Grade 11 or 12 students.
    3. Endorsement letter with school logo signed by the principal/director that the students and faculty advisor are the representatives of their respective schools.
    4. Accomplished parental consent and waiver form for minor students.
    You may download the parental consent and waiver form here: QQ Parental Consent Form
  3. Pay the PHP1,500 (per team) registration fee through GCash. You may upload your proof of payment in the registration form or email it to quakequest.upri@up.edu.ph. Kindly indicate [Registration - School name - Proof of Payment] in the subject line of your email.

    GCash Account Number: 09479720568

    MO***A M** M.

  4. Please wait for 3 to 5 working days for the confirmation of your registration after submitting the complete requirements. You will receive the physical copy of your receipt on the culmination day of the competition.
Note: Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis. Only the first 20 teams to complete their registration will be included in the official list of participants for the Quake Quest competition. Registration fee is non-refundable.

Important Dates to Remember

Start of Registration: July 1, 2024

Deadline of registration: August 2, 2024

Deadline of payment and completion of supplementary documents/requirements: August 9, 2024
