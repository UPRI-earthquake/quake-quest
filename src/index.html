Quake Quest

Competition Overview

In a span of 3 months, teams of high school students will compete to produce the best scientific project that utilizes the seismic data archive of UPRI’s public seismic network. This major quest will be divided into 4 stages spread out within the time duration: crash course series, abstract submission, documentation submission, and project presentation. In addition, a series of minor quests will be interspersed between the different stages of the major quest. These are intended to make the competition more fun and engaging for the participants. Every quest submission will be scored by points, and a leader-board ranking the different teams based on their live points will be made available online. Lastly, the competition will culminate with an on-site event for the project presentations, ‘fair’ game, and the awarding of winners. The target venue for the crash course series and the culmination event will be the CSRC and NISMED’s auditorium, respectively.

Target Participants

Senior High School Students and Faculties (Each team consists of 3 students and 1 faculty)
